# Project Requirements: 

## Project Requirements  
The project must deliver an AI system capable of predicting market trends and optimizing grease trap recycling operations using over 15 years of plant data, while also allowing for the integration of external data sources.  
— Predict market size and future grease collection volumes.  
— Identify patterns in outlet-specific data.  
— Provide insights through a user-friendly dashboard.  
— Optimize collection routes and scheduling for increased efficiency.  
— Predict which restaurants will miss cleaning in the current and next months.  
— Implement dynamic scoring for grading each establishment.  
— Forecast grease waste collection volumes for future months.  
— Generate intelligent schedules for inspectors.  
— Identify potential illegal grease trap waste dumping locations.  
— Analyze revenue fluctuations and their underlying causes.  
— Assess reductions in environmental impact due to proper dumping practices.  
— Develop methods for identifying non-legal cleanings.

## Project Understanding  
This project aims to enhance Envirol's grease trap recycling operations through AI, leveraging historical data to generate predictive insights that streamline processes, improve efficiency, and promote environmental compliance.  
— Focuses on increasing operational efficiency through predictive analytics and insights.  
— Aims to facilitate effective routing and scheduling for grease trap inspections.  
— Supports sustainability efforts in waste management by improving market understanding.

## Project Scope  
Develop and implement a web-based AI system with three distinct user roles: administrators, regular users, and guests. Administrators will have full control and access to all insights and data, regular users will utilize real-time data and reporting features, and guests will be able to access general information regarding grease trap recycling operations and market trends. The system will optimize processes and enhance predictive capabilities effectively.

# Key Objectives: 

 ## Key Objectives  
The key objectives are essential to ensure that the AI system effectively meets its intended purpose of predicting market trends and enhancing grease trap recycling operations. These objectives provide tangible outcomes that align with stakeholder needs and drive operational improvements.

- Predict which restaurants will miss cleaning in the current and next month with an accuracy of at least 85%.
- Implement dynamic scoring for each establishment to improve operational decision-making, with scores updated weekly and a minimum of 90% responsiveness to changes.
- Forecast grease waste collection volumes for the next 6 months with at least 90% accuracy, enabling proactive operational adjustments.
- Generate optimized smart schedules for inspectors to reduce travel time by at least 20% while maintaining coverage of all necessary locations.
- Identify potential illegal grease trap waste dumping incidents with a detection rate of 70% or better, facilitating timely interventions.
- Analyze and highlight revenue fluctuations, providing actionable insights into the top three contributing factors with a comprehensive report delivered monthly.
- Assess the reduction of environmental impact, aiming for a 25% increase in proper dumping rates compared to previous metrics within the first year of implementation.
- Develop methods to identify non-legal cleanings with a success rate of 75% in detection reliability, contributing to compliance and regulatory adherence.

## Best Practices for Grease Trap Maintenance

 

### Operator
**Grease Trap Maintenance**
*Actions*:
- Inspect grease traps regularly for signs of buildup or blockages.
- Schedule routine cleaning intervals based on trap usage and size.
- Remove accumulated grease and solids from the trap to prevent overflow.
- Record maintenance activities and monitor for recurring issues.
- Ensure that grease is properly disposed of according to environmental regulations.
- Check that trap components are functioning correctly and repair or replace damaged parts.
- Verify that grease trap effluent is flowing freely without backflow or leaks.
- Clean and disinfect the trap and surrounding area after maintenance.
- Educate staff on proper grease disposal practices to reduce trap overload.
- Implement preventative measures to minimize grease ingress and trap clogging.

### Maintenance Technician
*Actions*:
- Execute scheduled cleaning and inspection of grease traps.
- Use appropriate tools and methods to safely remove grease and solids.
- Perform troubleshooting for persistent clogs or malfunctions.
- Document cleaning procedures and maintenance outcomes.
- Ensure compliance with environmental and safety standards during maintenance.
- Assist in training personnel on correct grease trap upkeep practices.
- Assist in diagnosing issues related to trap efficiency and recommend solutions.
- Conduct tests to assess the quality of effluent post-maintenance.
- Maintain maintenance equipment and safety gear in optimal condition.
- Provide feedback on trap performance to improve maintenance protocols.
